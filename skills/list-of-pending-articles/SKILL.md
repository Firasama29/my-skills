Review the Cowork projects section and list every article discussed. Mark each one as **PENDING** unless the user has explicitly said it's done or archived.

Format the output as a numbered list:

1. [Article title or topic] — PENDING
2. [Article title or topic] — PENDING

If an article has been archived or completed, omit it from the list unless the user asks to see everything.
